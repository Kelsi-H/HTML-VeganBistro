About Us

We’re not your average leafy green bistro. Chive & Charm is all about big flavour, chill vibes, and food that you’ll never forget—no animal products needed. Born from a love of plants and a refusal to do boring, we remix comfort classics and whip up bold, colourful dishes that hit every craving. Feel good food with attitude: fresh, seasonal, and made to make you smile. Pull up a chair, bring a friend or two—this is where great taste meets good energy!
